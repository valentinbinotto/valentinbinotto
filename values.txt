### Values ###

# Respect, Equality, Transparency #

- We respect everyone regardless of physical or psychical characteristics or personal views and opinions. We engage in respectful behavior and treat each other in that same manner.
- We treat everyone equally, regardless of physical or psychical characteristics or social, political or other class-based positions.
- We are transparent about any mistakes and non-compliant behaviors. We do not hide information from others or withhold information in bad faith.
