Core Values - V434INFRANET.ORG

Respect
We treat everyone with respect. We listen to the ideas and opinions of others with due attention and openness. We interact with others in a kind, open and respectful manner.

Equality
We treat everyone equally. All have the exact same rights and freedoms. We do not discriminate against anyone on the ground of their opinions, beliefs or other characteristics.

Honesty
We are honest with each other. We disclose our mistakes and communicate openly. We stand up for our beliefs and values.
